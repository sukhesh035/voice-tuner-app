Privacy Policy

Effective: March 15, 2026

Swara AI is a vocal training application for Indian classical music. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: Email address and display name when you register.
  • Practice data: Session duration, score, note accuracy, and practice streaks. Raw audio is never stored.
  • Microphone access: Used in real time to detect pitch on-device. Audio is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous event data via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Device info: OS version, app version, and crash reports for debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the app (practice history, progress, streaks).
  • To enable classroom features when you join a teacher's classroom.
  • To send optional daily practice reminders if you enable notifications.
  • To improve app performance and develop new features using aggregated analytics.

3. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the United States, encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted to authenticated requests only.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: AWS (infrastructure), Firebase/Google (analytics).
  • Your teacher: If you join a classroom, your session summaries are visible to the classroom owner.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Microphone & Audio Data

Audio is processed locally on your device using on-device algorithms. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Swara AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us to remove any such data.

7. Your Rights

  • Access or correct the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking via your device settings.
  • Withdraw notification consent at any time in device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Deleting your account removes your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notification.
